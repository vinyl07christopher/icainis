Submission
Full papers should be submitted via,
  • email: icanisconf@gmail.com
    • Submission Guidelines

    Interested authors, researchers, and industrial experts are invited to submit their papers that best resonates with their technical background to the conference based on the research topics mentioned in Call for Papers section. If you have a paper on an additional topic, please write an email to icanisconf@gmail.com

    At the same time, contributors are encouraged to follow the below-mentioned guidelines and paper template to submit full-length papers to icanisconf@gmail.com

    Please note that a .docx and LaTex template is available to guide the authors in formatting their papers. All papers must comply with the template.

    General Guidelines

    Papers should be prepared in English and carefully checked for correct grammar.

    Figures should be of high quality. All papers must be submitted in electronic form to icanisconf@gmail.com

    Total number of pages must be 12-15 as per the template. Any paper exceeding this length will automatically be rejected.

    Review Process
    • To ensure high scientific quality, all papers will be reviewed by the Scientific Committee Members.
    • Once the reviewing process is complete, the contact author of each paper will be notified of the result by e-mail. The authors are required to follow the reviews in order to improve their paper before their final submission.
    Important Conference Policy
    • ICANIS reserves the right to exclude a paper from publication after the conference, if the paper is not presented at the conference.
    • The research contribution should not contain plagiarized material and have not been submitted to any other conference at the same time (double submission).
    • These matters are taken very seriously and ICANIS will take action against any author, who engages in either practice.
